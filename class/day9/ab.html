BMW M4

M4 COMPETITION

Engine: The BMW M4 is powered by a 3.0-liter M TwinPower Turbo inline-six engine. In the M4 Competition variant, it produces 530 hp (390 kW) and 650 Nm of torque.
Acceleration: The M4 Competition can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) in just 3.5 seconds thanks to its 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel-drive
